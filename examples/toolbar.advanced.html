TOOLBARS - PAGE 1

To have virtual 'pages' within a single document, you should declare the BODY or a DIV to have the 'data-role' attribute equal to 'pageview'. Within the page view you may declare the starting 'page' by setting the 'data-page' attribute to the ID of the 'page'. The pages' transition animation may be selected by setting the 'data-animation' attribute of the 'pageview'.

TOOLBARS - PAGE 2

Within a 'pageview' you define the pages - a DIV with 'data-role' equal to 'page'. Each 'page' is a 'container' as well - so you can directly put your controls in there.

Go to 3rd page

TOOLBARS - PAGE 3

Using the browser history buttons you may navigate through the pages. Also, by saving the URL you ensure that exactly that page will be loaded next time you type/paste that URL.
