DragOn example

The white square can be dragged only after tapping directly over it. Omitting the 'data-rect-constraint' property will allow the square not to be limited to the bounds of the parent DIV's rect.

The blue square can be dragged after tapping anywhere on the draggable region. Please note that the square may go outside the drag region by 10 pixels thanks to the 'data-rect-constraint' property.
