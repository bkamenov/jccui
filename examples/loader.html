Loader example

Below you can observe the 3 loaders available in JCC: spinner, screw and bars. The loader control will cover all the items of the parent element. It can be modal by visually darkening the contents of the parent or not but still preventing them from being accessed.



Implicitly modal 'spinner'-loader

The animation here is 'pop'.

Explicitly modal 'screw'-loader

The animation here is 'fade'.

Implicitly modal 'bars'-loader

The animation here is 'slideleft'.
